About CGC Hub

Empowering students with quality educational resources since 2025.

Our Mission

At CGC Hub, we're dedicated to breaking down barriers to education by providing free, high-quality study materials to students across all engineering disciplines. We believe that access to knowledge should be universal and unrestricted.

Our platform was born out of a simple idea: to create a centralized hub where students can find everything they need to succeed academically, without the hassle of searching through multiple sources.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted student resource platform, recognized for our comprehensive, up-to-date materials and commitment to academic excellence.

Our Values

Integrity, accessibility, and community are at our core. We're student-focused, constantly evolving to meet the changing needs of our users.

Department-Specific Resources
